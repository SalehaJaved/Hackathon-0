# Company Handbook

## Communication Rules
- All communications should be clear and respectful
- Use professional language in all interactions
- Respond to messages within 24 hours during business days
- Use appropriate channels for different types of discussions

## Financial Approval Rule
- Any expense above $100 requires prior approval from a manager
- Submit expense reports with receipts for reimbursement
- Keep records of all financial transactions

## Tone Guidelines
- Maintain a professional tone in all communications
- Be polite and courteous to colleagues and clients
- Keep messages concise and to the point
- Avoid jargon unless communicating with technical teams
