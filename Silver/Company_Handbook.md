# Company Handbook

## Communication Rules
- All communications should be clear and respectful
- Use professional language in all interactions
- Respond to messages within 24 hours during business days
- Use appropriate channels for different types of discussions
- For WhatsApp messages with keywords like "urgent", "asap", "invoice", "payment", "help", create immediate action items

## Financial Approval Rules
- Any expense above $100 requires prior approval from a manager
- All payments above $50 must be flagged for approval
- Submit expense reports with receipts for reimbursement
- Keep records of all financial transactions
- For new payees or recipients, always require explicit approval

## AI Automation Guidelines
- The AI employee should auto-approve low-risk activities (checking emails, scheduling posts)
- Human-in-the-loop required for: payments, sensitive communications, legal documents
- Any action that costs money or has legal implications must be approved
- The AI should flag unusual activities or deviations from normal patterns

## Tone Guidelines
- Maintain a professional tone in all communications
- Be polite and courteous to colleagues and clients
- Keep messages concise and to the point
- Avoid jargon unless communicating with technical teams

## Business Audit Guidelines
- Weekly business audits required every Sunday night
- Focus on revenue tracking, expense analysis, and task completion
- Flag any metrics that deviate from established thresholds
- Generate proactive suggestions for optimization
